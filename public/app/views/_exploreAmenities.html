Facilities

Whether you enjoy an active or relaxing lifestyle, Candlewood Lake offers plenty to do.

With facilities throughout the development, your favorite activities are only a few minutes away. The Candlewood Lake Office that houses the General Manager and other paid staff is conveniently located a short distance from the main gate on Candlewood Blvd. Next to the office is a Community center building that contains gently used exercise equipment available for use by all Candlewood members. The community has two lodges. The main lodge is located off Candlewood Drive and the RV Lodge is located in the RV area of the community.

The community has two swimming pools. Both pools have a pool house and food concession area. The pool located near the main lodge also has a kiddie pool. The second pool is located adjacent to the RV lodge.

Residents of Candlewood Lake have access to the community amenities and facilities. For questions regarding any facilities, contact the Candlewood Lake office (419) 947-1138 open Monday - Friday, 8:00am to 4:30pm.

In addition, the community has a:

  • Tennis court
  • Basketball court
  • Volleyball court
  • Beach
  • Playground
  • Picnic shelter
  • Picnic areas with tables and grills
  • Nature trail
  • RV camping and
  • Primitive Camp Sites

Lodges

Candlewood Lake has two lodges.

The Main Lodge is a two-story 7,000 square foot facility located on the waterfront. Each floor opens onto wrap-around decks offering breathtaking views of the lake in all seasons. The Lodge is centrally located adjacent to the main swimming pool and tennis courts. A temporary use boat dock is conveniently located below the lodge. Each floor contains a large stone fireplace with comfortable seating and an informal library is maintained by lake residents.

The Main Lodge upper and lower floors are each available for community and member use. The upper floor has an equipped kitchen making it convenient for catered events. In addition, the upper floor has a big screen television and other media available for gatherings and to help facilitate meetings.

The RV lodge, conveniently located in the RV Park, is 2,975 square feet under roof. It houses a pool table and a ping pong table. If desired, a wall of doors slide aside to create an outdoor pavilion-type atmosphere.

The RV lodge contains a kitchen and is also available for use by members of the community.

Both lodges are ideal settings for events such as: weddings, children's parties, adult parties, dinners, family reunions, club get-togethers, community meetings and gatherings, and more.
