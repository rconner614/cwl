Pools open around Memorial Day and close around Labor Day.

There are two swimming pools located within the community. The main pool, situated near the main lodge, recently underwent a renovation and is a popular place to be. The pool is 40' X 75' and includes a:

  • Diving board
  • Children's wading pool
  • Shower rooms with lockers
  • Lifeguards
  • A very popular concession stand

Swimmers and non-swimmers come to purchase lunch and snacks from the pool's concession all summer long.
