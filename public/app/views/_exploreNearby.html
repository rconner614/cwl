Candlewood Lake is located in Morrow County Ohio, just North of Mount Gilead and South of Galion, Ohio.

Columbus and Cleveland, Ohio are a short drive away. Closer cities such as Bucyrus, Lexington, Mansfield, Marion and Ontario are less than 25 minutes away and offer shopping centers with well-known groceries and department anchor stores, restaurants and entertainment.

Schools & Kids Activities

Candlewood Lake is located within the Northmor school district. Northmor is a newly built state of the art school campus housing grades kindergarten through 12. Children living within the Candlewood Lake community attend this campus which provides excellent academics and a variety of extracurricular activities.

To learn more about the Northmor school district, click on the page links or look for scholastic testing results at either eschoolprofile.com or greatschools.org.

There are also a variety of private and Christian schools within a short distance of Candlewood Lake. These links are provided as well. Kids have plenty of activities to keep them active while living in our community. Active Boy Scout & Girl Scout groups keep our young boys & girls busy both within and outside the Candlewood Community. Lake amenities such as swimming, the beach, basketball, tennis and beach volleyball also keep our kids active. The surrounding communities also provide organized sports and activities for children to enjoy. Science centers, museums, shopping and major league sports are a short driving distance from Candlewood Lake.
