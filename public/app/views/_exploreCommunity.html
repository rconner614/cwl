The Community


Candlewood Lake is a 1,500 acre planned, gated, serene lake community delivering advantages of city living in a recreational, vacation-like atmosphere year around.

Our private family living community offers something for all ages whether you enjoy an active or relaxing lifestyle. With facilities throughout the development, your favorite activities are only a few minutes away.

The current 750 full-time and part-time residences are nestled among our 1500 acre community with 140-acres dedicated to green space and amenities, and 25 acres set aside for parks.

The planned aspect of the community provides subdivisions set aside for single family housing, mobile homes, recreational vehicles or temporary camping.

The gated feature, in conjunction with Candlewood Lake's full-time security staff, provide the privacy you desire.

Utilities and Services we have come to rely on are available in our community facilitated by our full-time maintenance staff.

  • Community Water system
  • Community Sewer system
  • Trash removal
  • Road maintenance & snow removal
  • Maintenance of common areas

Utilities include:

  • High-speed Internet
  • Electricity
  • Natural gas
